Tossups

T1 2025 LITANY (2025) Diff 8 · Literature
This author wrote of a woman who is sent to Nonneseter [“NON-uh-setter”] on account of a rumor about her, her foster-brother, and the attempted rapist Bentein. A character created by this author violates custom by naming her child after her living husband. Simon Darre ends up with the protagonist’s sister Ramborg in a cycle by this author whose second book is set at the estate of (*) Husaby. Despite her pregnancy, the protagonist of a novel by this author wears the traditional virgin’s bridal crown during her wedding to Erlend Nikulausson. A cycle by this author that consists of the novels The Wreath, The Wife, and The Cross depicts the life of a nobleman’s daughter in medieval Norway. For 10 points, name this author of the trilogy Kristin Lavransdatter.
ANSWER: Sigrid Undset
T2 2022 MRNA VACCINE (2022) Diff 7 · Literature

In that novel, the narrator meets Ylajali [“ee-lah-yah-lee”] in a park and later decides to leave this country to join the crew of a ship. The protagonist of that novel from this country ponders eating his (*) pencil while succumbing to the title condition. For 10 points, name this home country of Knut Hamsun and the author of Ghosts, Henrik Ibsen.
ANSWER: Norway [or Kingdom of Norway; or Kongeriket Norge] (The first two sentences are from Sigrid Undset’s Kristin Lavransdatter trilogy.)
