Ideology and Ideological State Apparatuses

Althusser's 1970 essay (subtitled 'Notes towards an Investigation') introducing the concept of 'interpellation' — the process by which ideology turns individuals into subjects, illustrated by the example of a police officer 'hailing' someone on the street. It distinguishes between Ideological State Apparatuses (ISAs) — institutions like churches, schools, and the family that maintain ruling power nonviolently — and their 'repressive' counterpart (which exists as a single unified entity). The essay posits that no class can hold power without hegemony over its ISAs. It appears in the collection Lenin and Philosophy.

Summary of Facts

Louis Althusser is a French structuralist Marxist philosopher best known for his 1970 essay 'Ideology and Ideological State Apparatuses' and for co-writing Reading Capital. He is also notorious for killing his wife Helene Rytmann.

His most frequently clued work is the essay 'Ideology and Ideological State Apparatuses' (subtitled 'Notes towards an Investigation'), which introduces the concept of 'interpellation' — the process by which ideology transforms individuals into subjects. The key illustration is a police officer 'hailing' someone on the street, causing them to recognize themselves as a subject. The essay distinguishes between Ideological State Apparatuses (ISAs) — institutions like churches, schools, and the family that maintain ruling power nonviolently — and their 'repressive' counterpart, which exists as a single unified entity. It posits that no class can hold power without hegemony over its ISAs. This essay appears in the collection Lenin and Philosophy.

Reading Capital was co-written with students including Etienne Balibar and Jacques Ranciere, providing a structuralist reading of Marx's Capital. Ranciere later broke with Althusser over the 1968 student uprisings and championed 'dissensus' over consensus. E.P. Thompson wrote The Poverty of Theory as a lengthy criticism of Althusser's structuralist Marxism. Althusser was institutionalized after killing his wife Helene Rytmann.
