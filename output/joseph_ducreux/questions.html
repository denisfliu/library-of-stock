B1 2017 NASAT (2017) Diff 8 · Fine Arts
Just before he died, this artist made a chalk portrait of a black man who may be Toussaint Louverture. For 10 points each:
[10] Name this French artist who adopted unusual poses such as yawning in his self-portraits. A self-portrait in which he points at the viewer has become an Internet meme.
ANSWER: Joseph Ducreux (doo-KROO)
[10] Ducreux painted a portrait of this woman prior to her marriage. Jacques-Louis David sketched this queen on her way to the guillotine.
ANSWER: Marie Antoinette [or Maria Antonia Josepha Johanna]
[10] This female artist made several sympathetic portraits of Marie Antoinette, including one in which her son lifts the cover of a cradle of a dead relative. She depicted Lord Nelson's mistress, Lady Hamilton, as a Bacchante (buh-KONT) holding a tambourine.
ANSWER: Louise Elisabeth Vigée le Brun (vee-JAY luh BRUN) [or Madame Lebrun]
