     Leadin: Just before he died, this artist made a chalk portrait of a black man who may be Toussaint Louverture. For 10 points each:
  Part 1 (ans: Joseph Ducreux (doo-KROO)):
    Name this French artist who adopted unusual poses such as yawning in his self-portraits. A self-portrait in which he points at the viewer has become an Internet meme.
  Part 2 (ans: Marie Antoinette [or Maria Antonia Josepha Johanna]):
    Ducreux painted a portrait of this woman prior to her marriage. Jacques-Louis David sketched this queen on her way to the guillotine.
  Part 3 (ans: Louise Elisabeth Vigee le Brun (vee-JAY luh BRUN) [or Madame Lebrun]):
    This female artist made several sympathetic portraits of Marie Antoinette, including one in which her son lifts the cover of a cradle of a dead relative. She depicted Lord Nelson's mistress, Lady Hamilton, as a Bacchante (buh-KONT) holding a tambourine.
