Summary of Facts

Bharati Mukherjee is an Indian-Canadian-American author focused on displacement and immigration. Jasmine follows an Indian woman who kills Half-Face, immigrates to America, and settles in Iowa with Bud Ripplemayer. 'The Management of Grief' is inspired by the Air India Flight 182 bombing. Other works include Wife, The Tiger's Daughter, and The Holder of the World.
