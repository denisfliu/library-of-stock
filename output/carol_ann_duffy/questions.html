T1 2024 ARGOS (2024) Diff 9 · Literature
This author ended a poem by referencing Auden with the line, “Bring me the wine-cellar key. Let the less-loving one be me.” This poet wrote, “we stay trapped in time, / queuing for death,” in a poem which begins, “Thought of by you all day, I think of you.” A poem by this author which ends with the speaker stating “Long time since I walked / down Lonely Street / towards Heartbreak Hotel” is from a collection also containing a poem whose speaker recounts how her husband “held me upon that (*) next best bed.” Chiwetel Ejiofor starred in this author’s adaptation of Everyman. ANSWER: Carol Ann Duffy (The first poem is “Mrs. Beast.”)
