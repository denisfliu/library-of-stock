Summary of Facts

Amos Tutuola (1920-1997) was a Nigerian author who wrote in pidgin/non-standard English, drawing heavily on Yoruba folklore. The Palm-Wine Drinkard follows a protagonist who searches for his dead palm-wine tapster in the Dead's Town, encountering supernatural beings along the way. His mother proclaims 'this is what hatred did.' My Life in the Bush of Ghosts is his other major work.
