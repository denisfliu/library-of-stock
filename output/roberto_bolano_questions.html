ANSWER: The Obscene Bird of Night [or El obsceno pájaro de la noche]
[10] José Donoso, the author of The Obscene Bird of Night, was a novelist from this South American country, also home to Pablo Neruda and Nicanor Parra.
ANSWER: Republic of Chile
[10] This contemporary of Donoso described a search for Mexican poet Cesárea Tinajero in The Savage Detectives and penned the gargantuan novel 2666.
ANSWER: Roberto Bolaño Avalos
