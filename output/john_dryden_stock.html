Summary of Facts

John Dryden was the first English Poet Laureate under Charles II. Mac Flecknoe satirizes Shadwell. Absalom and Achitophel targets Monmouth and Shaftesbury. The Hind and the Panther defends Catholicism allegorically. His plays include All for Love and Marriage a la Mode. He translated Virgil and wrote Essay of Dramatick Poesie.
