T1 2022 ACF Winter (2022) Diff 7 · Fine Arts
This character hides under the “shade” of her “tree”-like father and takes a shower in an elevator in a 2020 Matthew Aucoin (“oh-KOYN”) opera adapted from a Sarah Ruhl play.
