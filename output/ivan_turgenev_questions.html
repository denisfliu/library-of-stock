T1 2024 Notes on the Underground (2024) Diff 10 · Literature

In another novel by this author, the thought of Alexander Herzen is satirized via the liberal character Gubaryov, who is encountered among a group of intellectuals at a spa in Baden-Baden. In a novel by this author, the widow (*) Marya tries to impress the Moscow official and poet Panshin with the piano playing of her daughter, whose name, Liza, sometimes titles translations of that novel. The unfaithful urban wife Varvara is contrasted with the title provincial group in a novel by this author focused on Lavretsky.
