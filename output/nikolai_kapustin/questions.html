ANSWER: Rhapsody in Blue
[10] This Frenchman arranged Django’s Nuages, Piaf’s pop, and Gillespie’s A Night in Tunisia, where he was born, for classical guitar. He composed Tango en Skaï (“on sky”) and Hommage à Villa-Lobos, dedicated Libra Sonatina to his heart surgeon, and started his recitals with improvisations.
