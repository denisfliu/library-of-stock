[T1] 2022 Chicago Open (2022) | Diff: 10 | Fine Arts
     Answer: Lady with an Ermine [or Dama con l'ermellino or Dama z gronostajem]
  - The earliest description of this painting is from a sonnet by Bernardo Bellincioni which opens by asking "Why are you angry? [PWR]
  - Whom do you envy, nature?" Using layer-amplification analysis, Pascal Cotte found that one of this painting's title figures was altered to be larger, lighter-colored, and more muscular, likely because it referred to a noble's nickname.
  - A 20th century restoration removed the footprint of an SS soldier from this painting. [PWR]
  - A 1497 letter requested that this painting be loaned to Isabella d'Este . [PWR]
  - Because this painting's background was repainted (*) black in the 18th century and its subject wears a thin, dark chain across the forehead, it resembles the artist's painting La Belle Ferronniere.
  - The Czartoyski  Museum houses this National Treasure of Poland, which depicts Ludovico Sforza's  mistress Cecilia Gallerani .
  - For 10 points, name this Leonardo portrait of a woman holding a short-tailed weasel. [GIVE]

[B1] 2023 MRNA (2023) | Diff: 7 | Fine Arts
     Leadin: The polished jet necklace of this painting's subject may be a reference to its patron's nickname of "the Moor" due to his dark complexion. For 10 points each:
  Part 1 (ans: Lady with an Ermine):
    Name this portrait of Cecilia Gallerini holding the title white animal that symbolizes her pregnancy with Ludovico Sforza.
  Part 2 (ans: Leonardo da Vinci [accept either underlined; accept Da Vinky?]):
    Lady with an Ermine is a portrait by this artist, whose only painting on public view in the Americas is his Portrait of Ginevra de' Benci. Lisa del Giocondo modeled for this artist's Mona Lisa.
  Part 3 (ans: ferronniere (ferr-on-NYER) [accept La Belle Ferronniere]):
    The Czartoryski (zar-tor-EE-skee) family gave Lady with an Ermine its name to differentiate it from a portrait of Francis I's mistress Lucretia Crivelli wearing this jewelry. These thin Renaissance headbands have a jewel in the center.
