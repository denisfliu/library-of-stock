  Part 2 (ans: du Maurier [or George du Maurier; or Daphne du Maurier]):
    Elizabeth Gaskell's episodic novel Cranford grew in popularity thanks to the illustrations of George, a cartoonist with this surname. His granddaughter with this surname set her novel Rebecca in her native Cornwall.
