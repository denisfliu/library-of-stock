Kobo Abe

← Home · View source questions · Make cards
Japanese surrealist/absurdist author (also known as Kimifusa Abe). Most commonly identified by his novel The Woman in the Dunes (featuring entomologist Niki Junpei trapped in a sand pit). Also known for The Face of Another, The Ark Sakura, Inter Ice Age 4, The Box Man, and his plays. Won the Akutagawa Prize for The Crime of S. Karuma. Collaborated with film director Hiroshi Teshigahara. Sometimes classified as magical realism.
