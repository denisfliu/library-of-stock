T1 2024 IQBT Undergraduate Championship (2024) Diff 8 · Literature
Some residents of this type of place are nicknamed Aimless, Feckless, Graceless, Pointless, and Big Business. Characters in a novel titled for this type of place include an artist working on a biography of Branwell Brontë and a woman who “saw something nasty in the woodshed” as a child. Aunt Doom and the Starkadders live in this type of place, to which Flora Poste travels to stay with her family in a novel by (*) Stella Gibbons. A novel titled for this type of place ends as a man and his neighbor both play an ace of spades in a card game; earlier, Clover had realized that six of seven commandments had been erased from a wall. One of these places named “Manor” is overrun in a rebellion led by Napoleon and Snowball. For 10 points, what type of place titles an allegorical George Orwell novel?
ANSWER: farms [accept Cold Comfort Farm; accept Animal Farm; prompt on dairies before “titled”]
