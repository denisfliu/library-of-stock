Summary of Facts

D.H. Lawrence wrote Lady Chatterley's Lover, Sons and Lovers, and Women in Love. His poetry includes 'Snake' (speaker hurls a log at a creature), 'Bavarian Gentians', and 'Piano.' Stories include 'Odour of Chrysanthemums' and 'The Rocking-Horse Winner.' He was a Georgian poet associated with Birds, Beasts and Flowers.
