T1 2024 This Set is Garbo (2024) Diff 10 · Fine Arts
A director with this name used a step printing effect for an enigmatic desert gunfight in which an ex-bounty hunter played by Warren Oates watches the death of his brother, also played by Warren Oates. A writer with this surname wrote a wartime film about Ukrainian guerillas that was re-edited in the 50s to reference the Hungarian Revolution, titled The North Star.
