T1 2025 Spec Script (2025) Diff 10 · Fine Arts

These two countries co-produced the notoriously erotic film (*) In the Realm of the Senses. A nonlinear 1959 film about these two countries is about an actress and architect known simply as “Her” and “Him.” For 10 points, name these two countries central to Hiroshima mon amour.
ANSWER: Japan AND France [accept Nippon or Nihon in place of “Japan”; accept French Republic or République française in place of “France”] (The films mentioned are Nagisa Oshima’s Max mon amour, Kiyoshi Kurosawa’s Serpent’s Path, Hirokazu Kore-eda’s The Truth, and Oshima’s Night and Fog in Japan.)
