    Name this artist who painted day and night versions of Boulevard Montmartre. His Apple Picking at Eragny and Bather in the Woods both show women in a rural setting.
  Part 2 (ans: impressionism):
    Pissarro exhibited as part of this movement in the middle of his career. It includes Manet and Monet, whose depiction of a sunrise scene shares its name.
  Part 3 (ans: Alfred Sisley):
    This artist's earliest surviving work is his Lane Near a Small Town. A British citizen, he is best known for works such as a View of the Thames and his Bridge at Villeneuve-la-Garenne.
