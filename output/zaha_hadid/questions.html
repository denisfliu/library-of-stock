T1 2022 NASAT (2022) Diff 8 · Fine Arts
A collaborator in this architect's firm used a term for self-generating systems to title the book The Autopoiesis of Architecture. Patrik Schumacher coined a term often used to describe this architect's Dongdaemun (dong-DAY-mun) Design Plaza, "parametricism."
