Tossups

T1 2024 IQBT Undergraduate Championship (2024) Diff 8 · Literature
A story by this author asks “Who has not seen the door in the wall,” using a fire door between number 20 and number 21 to start a philosophical paragraph about how “History could always have been otherwise.” Phil and his unnamed wife take a taxi to the Royal Athena Sun hotel in a story by this author, which ends by confirming that their driver ran over a child. This author of “Winter Break” used the phrases “rejoice” and “fucking rejoice” in a story in which an (*) IRA operative shoots a politician. This author wrote “The Assassination of Margaret Thatcher.” In an interview, this author said that Thatcher had a similar contempt of history as the protagonist of this author’s novel The Mirror and the Light. For 10 points, name this author whose trilogy about Thomas Cromwell includes Wolf Hall.
ANSWER: Hilary Mantel [or Hilary Mary Mantel; or Hilary Mary Thompson]
