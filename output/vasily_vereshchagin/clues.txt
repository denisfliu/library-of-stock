[B1] 2018 Cambridge Open (2018) | Diff: 8 | Fine Arts
     Leadin: In one painting, these people are composing a text which asserts that "the Devil shits and your army swallow it", and insults the recipient as "a Macedonian mechanic" and "a nasty glob of spit". For 10 points each:
  Part 1 (ans: Zaporozhian Cossacks [or Cossacks of Saporog]):
    Name this group of people, who in that work are seen laughing uproariously as they compose a reply to Mehmed IV's request for surrender.
  Part 2 (ans: Ilya Repin):
    The Reply of the Zaporozhian Cossacks is among the paintings that this Russian artist created as a tribute to his Ukrainian homeland. He also painted some rather tired labourers in Barge Haulers on the Volga.
  Part 3 (ans: Vasily Vereshchagin):
    This Russian contemporary of Repin depicted war in a rather more sombre and unflinching fashion in works like The Apotheosis of War, which shows a colossal pyramid of skulls in a desert.
