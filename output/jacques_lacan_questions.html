T1 2023 TELEOLOGY (2023) Diff 10 · Fine Arts
“The Coldness of Kubrick” and “Spike Lee’s Fantasmatic Explosions” are discussed in a Todd McGowan book subtitled for film “after” this thinker. This thinker dances to berimbau music after telling a Brazilian woman to go find her glass eye in a phone booth in a 2022 film in which he is played by David Patrick Kelly; that film is titled Adieu [this thinker]. This thinker, who delivers an offscreen lecture in the 2018 remake of Suspiria, influenced the adornments of the dance practice room in that film according to many critical readings. T2 2023 ACF Nationals (2023) Diff 9 · Philosophy
