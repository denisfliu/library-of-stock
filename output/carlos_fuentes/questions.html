Tossups

T1 2023 MRNA (2023) Diff 7 · Literature
In a short story by this author, a chef begins sexually desiring obese women before encountering a genie at a fast food restaurant. The protagonist of a novel by this author witnesses the Native American Tobias’s leg snap underneath a horse. A character created by this author leaves a ballroom of mirrors undamaged and encounters a character carrying a copy of Don Quixote in the desert. An illiterate character created by this author justifies his control using fake papers from the King of Spain and is General Tomas (*) Arroyo. A novel by this author ends as the secretary Padilla inherits an estate outlined in a will hidden from Teresa by a dying business tycoon. Harriet Winslow encounters a character created by this author based on Ambrose Bierce, who disappeared during the Mexican Revolution. For 10 points, name this Mexican author of The Death of Artemio Cruz and The Old Gringo.
ANSWER: Carlos Fuentes (The story in the first sentence is “El despojo” or “Spoils.”)

Sections titled “The Old World,” “The New World,” and “The Next World” divide a novel by this author that follows the construction of El Escorial under (*) Philip II. For 10 points, name this author of Terra Nostra, who fictionalized Ambrose Bierce’s foray into the Mexican Revolution in his novel The Old Gringo.
