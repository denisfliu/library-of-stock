B1 2025 COOT (2025) Diff 8 · Literature
Answer the following about some ace literature with asexual characters, for 10 points each:
[10] The aromantic and asexual Isaac Henderson is best friends with Charlie Spring in this graphic novel series created by Alice Oseman, who in 2020 opened up about her own asexuality.
ANSWER: Heartstopper
[10] A 2016 novel by this author sees the protagonist enter an asexual relationship with a man who claims “society has remained in the Stone Age”. This author created an eleven year old protagonist who meets a magician from the planet Popinpobopia.
ANSWER: Sayaka Murata(The novels are Convenience Store Woman and Earthlings.)
[10] An asexual author from this country never finished a novel about fishing, BAIT, before her death. A fictional stand-in for that author intervenes to stop an alcoholic from assaulting his mute son in the bone people, a novel from this country.
ANSWER: New Zealand(The author is Keri Hulme.)
