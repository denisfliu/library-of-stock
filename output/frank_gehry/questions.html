T1 2025 ACF Winter (2025) Diff 7 · Fine Arts
This architect designed the scenery for a 1968 Billy Al Bengston exhibition at LACMA. This architect fixed a full-scale F-104 jet fighter to the entrance of the California Aerospace Museum. This architect’s use of a fish motif appears in his design of a restaurant in Kobe. This architect’s “open-ended” approach can be seen in the use of corrugated steel and mesh fencing of his Santa Monica residence. A building designed by this architect described as a “metaphoric city” has an atrium he nicknamed “the flower.” This architect associated with Deconstructivism used concave wave-like exteriors for a Los Angeles concert hall. For 10 points, name this Canadian-American architect of the Walt Disney Concert Hall and Guggenheim Bilbao.
ANSWER: Frank Gehry [or Frank Owen Gehry; or Frank Owen Goldberg]
