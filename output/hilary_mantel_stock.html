Summary of Facts

Hilary Mantel wrote the Wolf Hall trilogy about Thomas Cromwell, winning the Booker Prize for both Wolf Hall and Bring Up the Bodies. The trilogy opens with Cromwell being beaten by his blacksmith father Walter. Bring Up the Bodies follows Cromwell's scheme to bring down Anne Boleyn. The Mirror and the Light ends with Cromwell's beheading. Her story 'The Assassination of Margaret Thatcher' ends with 'Fucking rejoice.'
