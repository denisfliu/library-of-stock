Marilyn Aronberg Lavin argued that a painting by this artist symbolizes the position of the sun on the winter solstice by positioning a dove and Christ’s hands and feet using Euclid’s formula for superimposing a pentagon on a triangle. Martin Kemp noted the “geometric control” of a painting by this artist, contrasting the octagonal pattern of the tiles inside the (*) loggia with the simpler grid pattern of the tiles outside the loggia.
