  Part 2 (ans: slave plantations):
    In Chesnutt's story "Mars Jeem's Nightmare," John tells McAdoo his grandson can no longer work at one of these places. In Colson Whitehead's The Underground Railroad, Caesar and Cora escape from laboring at one of these places.

    In this Chesnutt story, the Blue Veins Society member Mr. Ryder plans to marry a fellow mulatto woman, though he meets 'Liza Jane in a search for Sam Taylor.
