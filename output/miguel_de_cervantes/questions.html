[10] “The Lawyer of Glass” is one of the twelve stories in this Spanish series, which also includes “The Conversation of the Dogs” and “The Deceitful Wedding.” It was first printed in Madrid in the year 1613.
ANSWER: Exemplary Novels [accept “Novelas exemplares”]
[10] The Exemplary Novels were published by this author shortly before he completed his magnum opus, Don Quixote.
ANSWER: Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra
