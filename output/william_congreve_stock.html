Summary of Facts

William Congreve was a major Restoration playwright. The Way of the World features Mirabell and Millamant's famous 'proviso scene.' Love for Love has Valentine feigning madness. The Mourning Bride is the source of 'music has charms to soothe a savage breast' and 'hell hath no fury like a woman scorned.' He was a member of the Kit-Cat Club and friend of Jonathan Swift.
