T1 2025 The Pyramid (2025) Diff 10 · Fine Arts
Master IW’s rendition of this scene in their last attributed work borrowed the tent-filled background from its depiction in Cranach the Elder’s Law and Gospel. The central object of this scene is unusually thick and erect in Fyodor Bruni’s rendition, which was displayed next to Bryullov’s The Last Day of Pompeii.
