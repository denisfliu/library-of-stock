Summary of Facts

Pär Lagerkvist (1891-1974) was a Swedish Nobel Prize-winning author. His novel Barabbas begins with the crucifixion of Jesus and ends with the crucifixion of the title character. The Dwarf depicts its title character's life during the Renaissance.
