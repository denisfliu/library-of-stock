Summary of Facts

Anthony Trollope is a prolific Victorian novelist known for the Chronicles of Barsetshire and Palliser novels. The Warden follows Septimus Harding challenged by John Bold. Barchester Towers features Bishop Proudie, Mrs. Proudie, and Obadiah Slope. The Way We Live Now satirizes Augustus Melmotte's speculation scheme. He introduced mailboxes to England.
