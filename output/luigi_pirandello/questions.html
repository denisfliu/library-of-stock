ANSWER: Six Characters in Search of an Author [or Sei personaggi in cerca d’autore]
[10] This Italian Nobel Laureate and author of Right You Are (If You Think You Are) and Henry IV wrote Six Characters in Search of an Author
ANSWER: Luigi Pirandello
[10] This thematically similar play by Pirandello is about a company of actors led by Doctor Hinkfuss who puts on a play about Signora Ignazia and her four daughters Mommina, Totina, Dorina, and Nene.
ANSWER: Tonight We Improvise [or Questa sera si recita a soggetto]
