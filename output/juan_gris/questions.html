T1 2024 Notes on the Underground (2024) Diff 10 · Literature
In one novel, this character travels to South Africa, assassinates his daughter’s elderly guardian, organizes the lynching of the Black man Jupiter, and then releases hundreds of plague-ridden rats on a ship. John Ashbury’s introduction to a Penguin Classic about this character notes memorable scenes involving him battling a python and getting into a shootout among leaking wine barrels. This character, who was created by a pair of authors working as automotive journalists, robs a Russian princess of her pocketbook and jewels since she was too embarrassed to get out of the bath. In the first book titled for this character, he hacks a Marquise to death in her chateau and stuffs the body of an English Lord into a chest. This character’s daughter (*) Hélène is a love interest of the journalist Fandor, who assists Inspector Juve with tracking this character. Marcel Allain and Pierre Souvestre created this character, who appeared in 43 pulp novels and a series of crime films by Louis Feuillade. For 10 points, name this mask- and tophat-wearing master criminal from the popular fiction of Belle Epoque France.
ANSWER: Fantômas
