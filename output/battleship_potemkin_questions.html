Tossups

T1 2025 ACF Fall (2025) Diff 6 · Fine Arts
One of these objects partly titles a film in which an unmanly college student in love with Kitty King rolls through town on a hospital bed during a cyclone. A 1925 film titled for one of these objects shows a woman holding her trampled son in front of a line of troops.
