Summary of Facts

José Clemente Orozco was a Mexican muralist alongside Rivera and Siqueiros. The Epic of American Civilization at Dartmouth's Baker Library chronicles native peoples, Europeans, and industry, including the Departure of Quetzalcoatl and a skeleton giving birth atop books. Prometheus shows a man reaching for fire. The Trench depicts shirtless men. House of Tears compiled watercolors of prostitutes.
