T1 2024 Nora Barnacle Memorial Tournament (2024) Diff 10 · Literature

This author wrote a collection of essays on drama that coined the term “dramaturgy.” Odoardo kills his daughter to save her from the prince Hettore Gonzaga at the end of one of this author’s bourgeois tragedies, a genre he pioneered in his country. At the end of a play by this author, it is learned that a young (*) Templar is the sibling of Recha (“RAY-kuh”), the adopted daughter of the protagonist. After Werther’s suicide in The Sorrows of Young Werther, a copy of this author’s play Emilia Galotti is found laying open in his room. ANSWER: Gotthold Lessing [or Gotthold Ephraim Lessing] (The second line refers to Hamburg Dramaturgy. Doris Lessing is the other author in the giveaway.)
