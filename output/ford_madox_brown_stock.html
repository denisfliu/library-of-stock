Summary of Facts

Ford Madox Brown was an English Pre-Raphaelite painter. Work depicts laborers with Carlyle observing (took 13 years). The Last of England shows migrants under an umbrella before the White Cliffs of Dover. He painted the Manchester Murals. His grandson Ford Madox Ford wrote The Good Soldier.
