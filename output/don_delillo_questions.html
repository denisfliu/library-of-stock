Tossups

T1 2025 IQBT Undergraduate Championship (2025) Diff 8 · Literature
In a novel by this author, lyrical passages about the U-2 plane bookend a chapter describing the protagonist’s time in “the brig” in Atsugi, Japan. This author invented the characters of Win Everett and T. J. Mackey for a historical novel whose central event is reconstructed by archivist Nicholas Branch. In another novel by this author, Pieter Bruegel’s The Triumph of Death fascinates J. (*) Edgar Hoover, and Klara Sax attends a private screening of the Zapruder film. This author compares the Warren Commission report to Joyce’s Ulysses in a novel depicting the life of Lee Harvey Oswald. Nick Shay proclaims that “waste is the secret history” in a novel by this author that opens with the “shot heard ‘round the world” home run. For 10 points, Libra and Underworld were written by what postmodern American author, who also wrote White Noise?
ANSWER: Don DeLillo

In a novel by this author, Babette hides her use of the experimental drug Dylar from her husband, a professor of Hitler studies. T4 2017 Scattergories (2017) Diff 10 · Literature
