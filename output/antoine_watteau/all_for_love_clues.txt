[B1] 2016 MLK (2016) | Diff: 7 | Literature
     Leadin: In this play, Dolabella muses that "men are but children of a larger growth" upon being banished by his friend and romantic rival. For 10 points each:
  Part 1 (ans: All for Love):
    Name this reworking of Shakespeare's Antony and Cleopatra, in which the aides Alexas and Ventidius try to separate the lovers.
  Part 2 (ans: John Dryden):
    This first official poet laureate and author of All for Love allegorized contemporary England in his long poems The Hind and the Panther and Absalom and Achitophel, and poked fun at Thomas Shadwell in "MacFlecknoe."
  Part 3 (ans: Sir William Davenant [or William D'Avenant]):
    Dryden worked with this godson of Shakespeare on an adaptation of The Tempest titled The Enchanted Island. This author wrote the libretto to the first English opera, The Siege of Rhodes, and did not finish his epic poem Gondibert.
