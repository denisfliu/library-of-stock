T5 2019 ACF Nationals (2019) Diff 9 · Fine Arts

B. Andrews. Former Monty Python member Michael Palin was the first to interview the subject of these works, who originally worked as a nurse for Karl Kuerner. These paintings were primarily made in the loft of a millhouse in Chadds Ford, Pennsylvania, and in cover stories of both Time and Newsweek it was noted that the artist's wife wasn't aware of them being made.
