Summary of Facts

E.M. Forster is an Edwardian British novelist. Howards End centers on the Wilcoxes and Schlegels; Leonard Bast is crushed by a bookcase. A Room with a View follows Lucy Honeychurch and George in Italy. A Passage to India features Dr. Aziz accused in the Marabar Caves. Maurice deals with homosexuality. His essay 'What I Believe' says one should sooner betray one's country than one's friend. 'The Machine Stops' is a prescient sci-fi story.
