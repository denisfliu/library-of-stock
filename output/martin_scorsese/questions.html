T1 2022 MRNA VACCINE (2022) Diff 7 · Literature
In a novel primarily set in this city, a doctor reflects on how Nietzsche imitated Mucius Scaevola after Moji reminds him of how he sexually assaulted her at a party 20 years prior. The documentary The 50 Year Argument narrates the history of a magazine based in this city. The only surviving shop from this city’s old “Book Row” is the massive Strand Bookstore. This city names a company that often publishes previously untranslated and out-of-print novels under its “Classics" imprint, as well as an (*) oft-abbreviated literary magazine. One of several of this director’s films edited by Thelma Schoonmaker has opening credits that are accompanied by (*) Mascagni’s “Cavalleria Rusticana: Intermezzo.” The protagonist of that 1980 film looks into a mirror as he quotes Brando’s “I coulda been a contender” monologue from Elia Kazan’s On the Waterfront. ANSWER: Martin Scorsese [or Martin “Marty” Charles Scorsese]
