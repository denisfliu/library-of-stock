Turkey	Ancient Near East and Egypt	Modern name for Anatolia, home in antiquity to the Hittites, Lydia, Ionian Greek cities, and Troy.
Valerian	The Roman Empire and Late Antiquity	Emperor captured alive by the Sassanid king Shapur I, reportedly used as a human footstool, a humiliating first for Rome.

war elephants	Institutions, Society, and Material Culture	Battlefield animals used by Pyrrhus, Hannibal, and Hellenistic armies, notably at the Battle of the Hydaspes and Zama.
