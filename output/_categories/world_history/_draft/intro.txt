World History, as tested in quizbowl, spans the history of Africa, Asia, the Middle East, the Americas, and Oceania outside the narrower European and American History canons. East and South Asia dominate by sheer volume: Chinese dynasties from the Han through the Qing, Japan's shogunates and Meiji transformation, Korea's kingdoms, and the Indian subcontinent from the Mauryan and Mughal empires through the Raj and partition all recur constantly, alongside twentieth-century figures such as Mao Zedong, Deng Xiaoping, Jawaharlal Nehru, and Sukarno.

A second major cluster is the Islamic world and the Middle East, running from ancient Mesopotamia and the Achaemenid and Sasanian empires through the Rashidun, Umayyad, and Abbasid caliphates, the Ottoman and Safavid empires, and into the modern politics of Israel, Iran, Iraq, and the Arab world. Africa forms a third pillar, covering precolonial states such as Mali, Songhai, Great Zimbabwe, and Aksum, the European colonial partition, and the wave of twentieth-century independence movements and civil conflicts from Ghana to South Africa to Rwanda.

Latin America and the Caribbean supply a further dense layer of content: the Aztec, Inca, and Maya civilizations, Iberian conquest and colonial institutions, the wars of independence led by figures like Simon Bolivar, and the region's turbulent modern political history of revolutions, military juntas, and Cold War interventions in Cuba, Chile, Argentina, and Central America. Southeast Asia and Oceania round out the geographic coverage, from the Khmer and Majapahit empires to Vietnam's wars and Australia's and New Zealand's colonial and indigenous histories.

Cutting across all these regions are recurring answerlines that are not tied to a single place: European colonial trading companies and empires, the two World Wars and their global theaters, Cold War-era institutions like the Non-Aligned Movement, and the commodities, technologies, and social phenomena — gold, silver, silk, spices, slavery, gunpowder, famine, and disease — that recur as common threads through bonuses regardless of region.
