bridges	Civil & Structural Engineering	Structures spanning gaps like valleys or waterways, classified by design (beam, arch, suspension, cable-stayed) and analyzed for load-bearing capacity.
3D printers	Materials & Manufacturing	Additive manufacturing devices that build objects layer by layer from digital models, using materials like plastic filament or resin.
concrete	Materials & Manufacturing	Composite construction material of cement, aggregate, and water, reinforced with steel rebar for added tensile strength.
extrusion	Materials & Manufacturing	Manufacturing process forcing material through a shaped die to create objects with a fixed cross-sectional profile.
frequency	Electrical & Electronics Engineering	Rate of oscillation of a periodic signal, measured in hertz, central to circuit design and signal processing.
pistons	Mechanical Engineering	Cylindrical components that move within a cylinder to convert pressure into linear motion, central to internal combustion engines.
screws	Mechanical Engineering	Simple machines with a helical ridge, or thread, that convert rotational motion into linear force, used as fasteners.
stability	Civil & Structural Engineering	Property of a structure or system to return to equilibrium after a disturbance, a key consideration in structural design.
wind turbines	Energy Engineering	Devices converting kinetic wind energy into electricity via rotating blades connected to a generator.
