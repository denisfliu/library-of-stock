Stephen Sondheim	Sondheim's Musicals	Prolific Broadway composer-lyricist known for complex harmony and wordplay; wrote Company, Into the Woods, Sweeney Todd, and Sunday in the Park with George.
Cabaret	Bob Fosse's Musicals	Kander and Ebb musical set in Weimar-era Berlin's Kit Kat Klub, narrated by the sardonic Emcee amid the Nazis' rise.
Into the Woods	Sondheim's Musicals	Sondheim and James Lapine's musical weaving together fairy tales like Cinderella, Little Red Riding Hood, and Jack and the Beanstalk.
Andrew Lloyd Webber	Andrew Lloyd Webber and British Musicals	British composer of pop-influenced megamusicals including Jesus Christ Superstar, Cats, and The Phantom of the Opera.
Company	Sondheim's Musicals	Sondheim's concept musical about a single man, Bobby, examining marriage through vignettes with his married friends.
Lin-Manuel Miranda	Contemporary and Hip-Hop Musicals	Composer-lyricist-performer who wrote and starred in Hamilton and In the Heights, blending hip-hop with Broadway tradition.
West Side Story	Golden Age and Dance-Driven Broadway	Bernstein and Sondheim musical relocating Romeo and Juliet to rival New York gangs the Jets and Sharks; choreographed by Jerome Robbins.
A Chorus Line	Golden Age and Dance-Driven Broadway	Michael Bennett's musical following Broadway dancers auditioning and sharing personal stories at a single callback.
Chicago	Bob Fosse's Musicals	Kander and Ebb musical satirizing celebrity and the justice system through murderesses Roxie Hart and Velma Kelly; choreographed by Bob Fosse.
Jesus Christ Superstar	Andrew Lloyd Webber and British Musicals	Lloyd Webber and Tim Rice's rock opera retelling the last week of Jesus's life from Judas's perspective.
Phillipa Soo	Contemporary and Hip-Hop Musicals	Actress who originated the role of Eliza Hamilton in Lin-Manuel Miranda's Hamilton.
Pippin	Bob Fosse's Musicals	Stephen Schwartz musical about Charlemagne's son seeking meaning in life, staged with Fosse's signature choreography and a manipulative Leading Player.
