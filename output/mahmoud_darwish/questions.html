T1 2025 ACF Nationals (2025) Diff 9 · Literature
A poem addressing this city remarks, “The geography of my body changes. / The cells of my blood become green. / My alphabet is green.” “Poems become diaphanous (“dye-AFF-uh-nuss”)” in this city in a poem titled for its “Collar of the Dove” that provides the title of the collection The Butterfly’s Burden. ANSWER: Damascus [or Dimašq; accept al-Shām; accept “Damascus, What Are You Doing to Me?” or “The Damascene Collar of the Dove” or Songs of Mihyar the Damascene] (The Butterfly’s Burden is by Mahmoud Darwish.)
