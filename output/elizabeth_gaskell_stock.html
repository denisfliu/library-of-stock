Summary of Facts

Elizabeth Gaskell wrote North and South (Margaret Hale moves to industrial Milton and marries Thornton), Cranford (Miss Matty goes bankrupt), Mary Barton, Ruth, and a biography of Charlotte Bronte. She serialized works in Dickens's Household Words.
