B1 2020 Longhorn Invitational Tournament (2020) Diff 7 · Literature
This place appears twice in the title of a poem that contains the questions “Say, who are you that mumbles in the dark?” and “Who said the free? Not me?” For 10 points each:
[10] Name this place. The narrator of another poem shows his loyalty to this place by promising to “be at the table / When company comes.”
ANSWER: America [or the United States of America; or USA; accept “Let America Be America Again” or “I, Too, Sing America”]
[10] This author of “Let America Be America Again” and “I, Too, Sing America” was a major figure of the Harlem Renaissance. His poem “Harlem” asks “What happens to a dream deferred?”
ANSWER: Langston Hughes [or James Mercer Langston Hughes]
[10] In this Langston Hughes poem, the speaker writes “I am the only colored student in my class” after an “instructor” tells him “Go home and write / a page tonight.”
ANSWER: “Theme for English B”
