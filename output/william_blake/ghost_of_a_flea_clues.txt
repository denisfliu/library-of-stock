[B1] 2012 Minnesota Open (2012) | Diff: 10 | Fine Arts
     Leadin: The "Beast from the sea" is shown rising up from between this figure's legs in one painting, and this figure is also the subject of the painting "The Number of the Beast is 666." For 10 points each:
  Part 1 (ans: The Great Red Dragon):
    Name this figure with enormous wings and many heads that is shown with a woman being "Cloaked in the sun" in one entry of a series of paintings based on Revelation 12.
  Part 2 (ans: William Blake):
    The Great Red Dragon series was by this painter who also made many depictions Urizen doing things like kneeling at an altar before the Demiurge. He also created a series of engravings that illustrated the Book of Job and his own Songs of Experience.
  Part 3 (ans: The Ghost of a Flea):
    This tiny Blake work shows the title creature, which has a tiny head atop a huge neck, walking across what may be the stage of a theater. Its title creature is sticking its tongue out toward a big bowl that is presumably holding blood.
