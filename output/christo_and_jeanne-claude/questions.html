B1 2023 ACF Fall (2023) Diff 6 · Fine Arts
For his Anthropometries series, Yves Klein painted nude models this color and had them make imprints on a blank canvas as “human paintbrushes.” For 10 points each:

ANSWER: blue [accept International Klein Blue]
[10] Klein created a headless plaster cast of this sculpture completely covered in blue. Alexander of Antioch is believed to have created this armless sculpture that features 28 times in Dalí’s The Hallucinogenic Toreador.
ANSWER: Venus de Milo [or Aphrodite of Melos]
[10] These two artists were both part of the Nouveau réalisme movement founded by Yves Klein. This husband-and-wife duo designed large landmark projects such as the Wrapped Reichstag.
ANSWER: Christo and Jeanne-Claude [or Christo Vladimirov Javacheff and Jeanne-Claude Denat de Guillebon; prompt on partial answer]

A painting in another series from this country shows a man in all black in a courtroom with a red-and-white checkered floor; that painting was completed at the Heide farm, which names this country’s Heide Circle of painters.
