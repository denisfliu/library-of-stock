T1 2024 IQBT Undergraduate Championship (2024) Diff 8 · Literature
In a story by this author, a man returns a letter from a dead friend to a woman living in Rincon Hill, but she callously tosses the letter into the fire when she sees that it is stained with blood. A character created by this author crosses a brook using some boulders before running in fear from a long-eared rabbit. This author wrote a story in which a man observes “great golden stars... in strange constellations” as he walks along a densely-wooded road to return to his home. (*) “Killed at Resaca” appears in this author’s Tales of Soldiers and Civilians, as does a story in which a deaf-mute boy with a wooden sword encounters his home in flames. For 10 points, name this author of “Chickamauga” who wrote about the execution of Peyton Farquhar in “An Occurrence at Owl Creek Bridge.”
ANSWER: Ambrose Bierce [or Ambrose Gwinnett Bierce]
