T1 2020 DECAMERON (2020) Diff 7 · Literature
This story’s final line notes an “open space” between what a man “knew” and “tried to believe” that “nothing could be done about.” A man in this story pins a postcard to a blood-stained shirt in honor of a character he thinks was killed with a tire iron. After discussing leaving for Mexico, a man in this story says “I wish I knew how to quit you.” In this story, Alma Beers divorces her husband after noticing that he doesn't use his pole on fishing trips. As teenagers, this story’s protagonists work for Joe (*) Aguirre at the title location, where they take turns cooking meals and herding sheep. Larry McMurty won an Academy Award for co-writing a film adaptation of this story, which is collected in 1999’s Close Range: Wyoming Stories. For 10 points, name this story by Annie Proulx about Jack Twist and Ennis del Mar’s homosexual love affair, which was adapted into an Ang Lee film.
ANSWER: “Brokeback Mountain”
