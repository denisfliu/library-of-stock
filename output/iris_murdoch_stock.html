Summary of Facts

Iris Murdoch is an Irish-born British novelist and philosopher. Under the Net follows Jake Donaghue kidnapping the dog Mr. Mars. The Sea, The Sea won the Booker Prize and features Charles Arrowby. The Bell is set at Imber Court. The Black Prince follows Bradley Pearson. She suffered from Alzheimer's; John Bayley wrote Elegy for Iris.
