T1 2023 Penn Bowl (2023) Diff 8 · Literature
This author took inspiration from tapestries in Paris' Cluny Museum for a novel in which Hannah accepts her imprisonment inside Gaze Castle after pushing her lover off a cliff. A character in a novel by this author blames the young Titus' drowning on the husband of a childhood crush he attempts to kidnap away from him. In a novel by this author, a man who volunteered to test possible cures for the cold worries that he's violating the privacy of his roommate by adapting their conversations into a book inspired by (*) Wittgenstein. This author wrote a novel in which a former dramatist obsesses over Mary Hartley Fitch while exiling himself in a cottage in the fictional coastal village of Shruff End. For 10 points, name this author of Under the Net, who created Charles Arrowby in The Sea, The Sea.
ANSWER: Iris Murdoch [or Dame Jean Iris Murdoch]
