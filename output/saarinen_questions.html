T1 2023 ILLIAC (2023) Diff 7 · Fine Arts

This architect set out to design “the simplest skyscraper in New York City” for a building whose facade of dark granite piers led it to be nicknamed “The Black Rock.” The CBS Building was designed by this architect whose Miller House is credited with popularizing the conversation pit. A structure designed by this architect that was nicknamed (*) “the whale” for its sloping roof is the David S. Ingalls Rink at Yale.
