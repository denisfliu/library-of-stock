Thomas Cole

View source questions · Make cards
British-American landscape painter, founder of the Hudson River School. Most commonly identified by The Oxbow, The Course of Empire (5-painting series), and The Voyage of Life (4-painting series). Patron: Luman Reed. Depicted alongside William Cullen Bryant in Asher Durand's Kindred Spirits. Also painted The Titan's Goblet and The Architect's Dream.
The Course of Empire (1833–1836)

Series of five paintings depicting the rise and fall of a civilization. Commissioned by Luman Reed. Inspired by a line from Byron's Childe Harold's Pilgrimage. Each painting depicts the same rocky outcrop with a precariously balanced boulder. Wikipedia
