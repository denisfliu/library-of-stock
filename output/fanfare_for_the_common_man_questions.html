Questions: Fanfare for the Common Man

1 tossups · 9 bonuses

Tossups

T1 2025 MRNA (2025) Diff 7 · Fine Arts
The New York State Theatre’s opening ceremony featured an antiphonal performance of an Igor Stravinsky piece in this genre. In 1942, the Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra premiered 18 pieces in this genre commissioned by Eugene Goosens, including one “for American Heroes” by William Grant Still. Conductor Marin Alsop is the dedicatee of a piece in this genre by Joan Tower, whose title alludes to an earlier piece in this genre which opens with timpani, bass drum, and tam-tam, followed by (*) trumpets playing the ascending notes F, B-flat, F. Leo Arnaud’s “Bugler’s Dream” is in this genre and is used for television presentations of the Olympic Games. Elongated horns designed to hang banners name, for 10 points, pieces in what genre, one of which Aaron Copland wrote “for the Common Man”?
ANSWER: fanfares [accept fanfarade; accept flourish; accept Fanfare for American Heroes; accept Fanfare for a New Theatre; accept Fanfare for the Uncommon Woman; accept Fanfare for the Common Man]
