T1 2022 C++ (2022) Diff 7 · Fine Arts
In a painting by Artemisia Gentileschi, a figure allegorized as this concept sits with half-opened eyes near a darkened perfume bottle. In a painting titled for this concept, two infants use sticks to pass a large ball through a wooden hoop. The seven rungs of a ladder frame a town in the background of a work named for this concept, which depicts a bat-like creature holding up a banderole displaying its title. A rainbow appears overhead in that work above a (*) truncated polyhedron nicknamed its artist’s “solid.” In Renaissance art, this concept was traditionally depicted by a woman resting her hand on her chin, as seen in a 1514 “master engraving” that includes the symbols of an hourglass, weighing scales, and a magic square. For 10 points, what concept precedes I (“one”) in the title of a print by Albrecht Dürer?
ANSWER: melancholy [or Melencolia I; prompt on acedia] (The second line refers to the painting by Lucas Cranach the Elder)
