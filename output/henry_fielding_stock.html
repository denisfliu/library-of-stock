Summary of Facts

Henry Fielding is an 18th-century English novelist. Tom Jones is about a foundling raised by Squire Allworthy who marries Sophia Western. Shamela parodies Richardson's Pamela. Joseph Andrews features Parson Adams and Joseph's strawberry birthmark. He wrote plays including Tom Thumb before the Licensing Act. He founded the Bow Street Runners. His sister Sarah wrote The Governess.
