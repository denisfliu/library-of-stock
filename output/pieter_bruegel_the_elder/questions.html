ANSWER: hunts [or hunting; accept fox hunts, wolf hunts, boar hunts, lion hunts, tiger hunts, hippopotamus hunts, or crocodile hunts; accept Charles I at the Hunt; accept Hunters in the Snow]
T2 2014 ACF Regionals (2014) Diff 8 · Fine Arts
