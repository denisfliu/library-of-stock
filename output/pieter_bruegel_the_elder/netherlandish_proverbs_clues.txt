     Answer: Netherlandish Proverbs [or Nederlandse Spreekwoorden; accept The Blue Cloak until read] (Copia is a treatise by Erasmus.)

  - This painting [emphasize] does not include written words, a departure from a similar print by Frans Hogenberg.
